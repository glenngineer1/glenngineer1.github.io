About Glenn

Glenn moved to Nashville in 1993 and worked as a recording engineer for many of the top artists in the Nashville area. In 2010, he moved to Daytona Beach to teach at Daytona State College in their Music Production and Technology Department. He was responsible for creating curriculum on his own in line with state required course outcomes. Throughout his engineering and teaching career, Glenn always dedicated himself to his clients and students to help them achieve their goals. Problem solving is one of his greatest assets as he tries to find the best solution to what's in front of him.

Glenn is an avid hockey fan and a big follower of the Nashville Predators. He has written columns for Along the Boards about the Predators. When not following or writing about hockey, Glenn enjoys hiking at Radnor Lake, ping pong, cooking, traveling and spending time with his family.
